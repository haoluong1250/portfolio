A smart stroller for safer travels

The stroller design has been around for decades with little innovations. They are low-tech, unsecure, and not suitable for usage in the dark. By integrating mobile connectivity, Barnvagn empowers parents to remotely control and customize a range of features, ensuring convenience and peace of mind like never before.

Need Finding

Observation

I conducted the observations for user need finding at UTC Shopping Center, where parents often push their babies in stroller as they browse around the mall. Notes were written down, photos were taken, and sketches were drawn.


1. Mother distracting a crying baby

2. Father checking in on baby

3. Leaving luggage unattended

4. Hanging bags on the handlebar

Observed Needs

User needs to ...

  • Entertain/distract their child
  • Check in on their child
  • Carry and protect their luggage
  • See when it gets dark

User Testing

Method

I user tested with five tasks a typical user might complete. Then, users were asked to complete a survey. Lastly, I critiqued my design based on Shneiderman's 8 Golden Rule.


Tasks:

  • Find the instructions on how to connect a stroller
  • Connect to the stroller using the manual pin input method
  • Turn on the headlights
  • Unlock the storage compartment
  • Disconnect the stroller
Survey Questions 
Findings
  • Standardize upper/lowercases, buttons, and links
  • Add confirmation for action items
  • Label the cards of widgets on the home page

Web Development

Try it out!

I built the home page using HTML/CSS, JavaScript, and BootStrap 5's framework.

Instructions:

  1. Press the disconnect button to open an overlay, confirming your disconnection. This prevents the potential mistake of disconnecting accidentally.

  2. In the music player: press the pause/play, the forward, and backward button. Also adjust the volume by using the slider, or press each speaker icon.

  3. Adjust the brightness of the lights by moving the slider. Or press on each of the lightbulb for minimim or maximum adjustments.

  4. Press the lock button to lock or unlock the stroller.

  5. Scroll to the bottom and press "home" on the header to return back to the top.
