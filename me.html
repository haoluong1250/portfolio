me




Hi there! My name is HAO LUONG and I am a 3rd year UX Design student at UC San Diego studying Cognitive Science specialized in Design and Interaction and a minor Design. My three plus years of digital design experience ranges from designing charity shopping websites to apps that inspire healthy life habits. Although my studies occurs in San Diego, I am also based in the Bay Area!

My studies at UC San Diego have provided opportunities of engagement in classes that examines the consequential effects of technology, how to conduct and statistically analyze field and research methods, understanding the neurobiology of cognition, etc. I have also taken classes that are essential in the UX Design field such as programming courses like Python and JavaScript's and linear algebra.

Outside of my studies, I am an active member in Design Co, an on campus design club that fosters a community of opportunities between industry designers and students. I continually focus on my career, seeking to expand my network, participate in group/real-world design projects, to gain first-hand experience and improve on my skills.
